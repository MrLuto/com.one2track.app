Track your One2Track GPS watch in Homey Pro with live status, location updates, messaging and safety alerts. The app supports multiple trackers on one account and only shows controls that your watch model actually supports.

Your One2Track credentials are stored on Homey to keep a shared session active for paired devices. Risky commands such as remote shutdown, factory reset and password changes are disabled by default, and WIFI is treated only as tracker status, not as Homey presence.
